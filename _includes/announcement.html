Resuming in-person 2023

Dear Software Crafters,

We are happy to announce that we are returning to an in-person event for 2023.

COVID-19 rates across Europe are still high. We want to keep attendees safe, while at the same time allowing for as much familiarity and community spirit as is possible.

We will monitor the situation in the UK and across Europe closely, and will make our mask and vaccination policy clear by 1st March 2023. At a minimum, our policy will meet the legal requirements in force in the UK at the time of the conference.

Regardless of whether we decide that mask wearing will be required at the conference, we commit to supporting the decision of anyone who wishes to wear a mask.
