The cost to attend is £725, which includes your accommodation and meals from Thursday diner til Sunday lunch.

For those who wish to participate in the training day on Thursday, an additional fee of £358 applies.

If you would like to arrive at the venue the day before the event starts on Wednesday, you can pay an extra £110 for your Wednesday night accommodation, dinner, and breakfast.

We are constantly searching for sponsorships that could lower the cost of tickets, and if successful, we will distribute the savings evenly among all attendees by issuing a refund for the difference after the event.
